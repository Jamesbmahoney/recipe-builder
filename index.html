Fridge Raider

Because Takeout Was So 2021...

We have all been there...

Picture this. You are starving enough that your roommate starts to looks appetizing, but you have already spent so much money on takeout that you could make a mosaic out of fast food wrappers. This doesn't put your mind at ease or fill your stomach. In fact you are even hungrier and even a bit on edge now. "WHY MUST I BE SO HUNGRY AND SO BROKE!?", you scream out loud. You sigh and begrudgingly open the refrigerator, but alas there doesn't seem to be a coherent yet delicious meal in front of you.

That's where we come into the picture!

Fridge Raider is a service that allows you to load in a random assortment of fridge items: those 4 eggs, the onions, maybe the leftover ham, and in return shoots out a helpful recipe for an interesting meal everytime!

Don't forget the drinks!

All of that stress over what to eat has made you crave an adult beverage. Why not have a go at the random drink button? Each time you click "Get Drink", it will give you a random and tasty drink to mellow out your overworked mind. 😊
